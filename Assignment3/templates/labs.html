Labs

Welcome to CSCB20 Labs! Each week, you'll participate in hands-on practical sessions that reinforce the concepts covered in lectures. Labs are designed to give you direct experience with databases and web development technologies. You'll work on exercises ranging from SQL queries to building web applications, with teaching assistants available to help guide you through the material. Labs are an essential part of your learning and contribute to your final grade. Remember to come prepared by reviewing the lecture material beforehand.
